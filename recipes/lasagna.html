Lasagna

Worlds Best Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Make and cook the lasagna noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna in the order: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese, repeat.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes covered and then 25 minutes uncovered at 375 degrees F.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
